About Me

A brief introduction about myself, my background, skills, and passions.

Background & Education

I’m a computer science student with a strong interest in building clean, reliable web applications. Over the past year I’ve focused on hands-on projects that combine front-end design with back-end data handling, emphasizing usability and maintainability.

My coursework and personal projects have given me a solid foundation in programming, problem-solving, and version control. I enjoy translating ideas into real products and documenting my process along the way.

Skills & Technologies

I work primarily with modern web technologies and common developer tools. I’m comfortable turning wireframes into responsive interfaces and connecting them to secure back-ends.

  • Front-End: HTML5, CSS3 (Flexbox/Grid), JavaScript
  • Back-End: PHP, MySQL (CRUD, authentication, sessions)
  • Tools: Git & GitHub, VS Code, Linux basics

Hobbies, Passions & Goals

Outside of coding, I enjoy learning new frameworks, exploring UI patterns, and contributing to small community projects. I’m passionate about accessible, consistent design that scales.

My short-term goal is to strengthen my full-stack skills and ship more portfolio-ready features. Long-term, I aim to contribute to impactful web platforms that improve everyday workflows and user experience.
